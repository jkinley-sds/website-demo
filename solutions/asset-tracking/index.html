The Challenges Within Asset Tracking

In todayâ€™s fast-paced business world, keeping track of all the moving pieces can be increasingly frustrating and expensive. Accurately watching over the location, possession and movement of valuable company assets can be the difference in finishing a job on time or missing costly deadlines. From hospitals to construction sites, facility management, and equipment sales, forward-thinking companies are looking for better ways to track valuable equipment.

As we with work with clients in looking to better handle this process, we continuously find ways to use mobile technology to give them greater clarity into the real-time activity of all critical assets. The simplicity of attaching barcodes to equipment and using mobile devices to capture the movement of these assets saves companies money, time and the embarrassment of misplaced goods. Additionally, back-office workers can assign or schedule equipment to employees, locations or customers giving everyone on your team up-to-date information on all asset movement.

What Mobility Can Do For Asset Tracking

By implementing a forward-thinking, mobile asset tracking solution, our clients have been able to:

  • Lower the costs associated with lost or misplaced assets
  • Identify owner status, last location, and availability of returnable equipment
  • Assign or schedule equipment to future jobs and workers
  • Give field workers the ability to update asset movement right from a mobile device
  • Provide verifiable chain of custody with signature capture
