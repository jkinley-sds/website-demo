Warehouse Management

Keep the supply chain moving forward by directing the critical movement of all inventory within your warehouse.

Features

Having a reliable and comprehensive Warehouse Management System is essential for any production or distribution company’s success. Leverage our built-in warehouse management functionality, or integrate with a dedicated enterprise WMS for even greater visibility into the movement and storage of your inventory.

  • Cross Docking

    Free up wasted time by giving your employees the ability to receive and ship product by eliminating the put-away, storage, and selection process.

  • Item Warehousing

    From the moment you receive an item into your warehouse until it leaves through the dock, take control of every important exchange.

  • Loading Procedures

    Optimize your staging, kitting and loading process by tracking the movement of warehouse inventory.

  • Will Call

    Quickly designate inventory to be staged for will call keeping your warehouse prepared and organized for customer pickup.

  • Defect Tracking

    Obtain a sharper focus on your assembly and storage operations by marking out inventory as defective or damaged and track it through the repair process if needed.

  • Sub-Assembly Tracking

    Identify and track stockable items that have gone through an assembly process, but will also be needed in the assembly of other items.

  • Container Tracking

    Cut out the wasted energy and costs associated with hunting down returnable containers by leveraging the built in asset tracking feature.

  • WMS Integration

    Integrate with a dedicated enterprise WMS for even greater visibility into the movement and storage of your inventory.

  • Item Staging

    Give your production and logistics departments greater clarity into all items on an order and prepare inventory for staging before shipment scheduling.
