Driver Management

Give your mobile workers the leg-up they need to be equipped for each day and your customers the quality of service they expect.

Features

Nothing ruins an efficient day like forgotten supplies or neglected safety checks. Take advantage of tailored mobile workflows and GPS management to keep your fleet moving forward.

  • Pre/Post Trip Checklists

    Ensure that each driver has route specific supplies or product, perform vehicle safety checks and enforce end of route checklists customized to your business rules.

  • Trip Tracking

    Give your operations team the visibility they need into individual drivers or the entire fleet by knowing the total time of a route, the time spent at stops or in transit, and comparing odometer to GPS mileage.

  • GPS Tracking

    Use built in GPS technology to locate, plan and direct your mobile workforce. Identify and assign jobs to the proper mobile workers and keep the supply chain moving.
